Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you might want to know

Organized by topic. Can't find your answer? Reach out and we'll get back to you within one business day.

Scheduling

How far in advance should I book?+

One-time cleans can often be scheduled within a few days; recurring plans usually start within a week. Peak seasons (spring, move-season) may need a bit more lead time.

Can I reschedule or skip a visit?+

Yes — just give us at least 48 hours' notice and we'll adjust your schedule with no penalty.

Payments

What payment methods do you accept?+

We accept all major credit cards and ACH bank transfer. Invoices are sent after each visit, or automatically for recurring plans.

Do you require a deposit?+

One-time and specialty cleans (move-out, post-construction) may require a small deposit to hold your slot; recurring plans do not.

Cleaning Supplies

Do I need to provide any supplies?+

No — we bring everything needed, including non-toxic, biodegradable products and equipment.

Can you use my own products or a specific brand?+

Absolutely. Let us know in your quote request or notes and we'll accommodate your preference.

Cancellation Policy

What's your cancellation policy?+

Cancel or reschedule with at least 48 hours' notice at no charge. Late cancellations may incur a partial fee to cover the reserved crew time.

Insurance

Are you insured?+

Yes — ProntoCleaning Solutions carries general liability insurance, and all staff are background-checked before joining a crew.

Pets

Is it okay if my pets are home during the clean?+

Yes, most pets do great. Just let us know in advance so our team can plan around them, and all our products are pet-safe.

Safety

What safety measures do you follow?+

Background-checked staff, insured service, and non-toxic products across every job — the same standard whether it's a home or a clinic.

Our team is happy to walk through the details before you book.
