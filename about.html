Who We Are

Fast. Thorough. Green.

ProntoCleaning Solutions was built on a simple belief: a clean environment shouldn't come at the cost of a healthy one.

Our Story

Founded in Nairobi, 2026

ProntoCleaning Solutions started with a two-person crew and a simple standard: show up on time, use nothing that could harm a family or the planet, and leave every space genuinely clean — not just tidy. Eight years later, that standard hasn't changed, even as we've grown to serve homes, offices, clinics, and rental properties across the entire Twin Cities metro.

We deliver residential and commercial cleaning services that are fast, reliable, and 100% eco-conscious — the same promise on day one as it is today.

Mission & Vision

Clean spaces. Calm minds. Every time.

Mission — To give every client back their time and peace of mind through dependable, eco-conscious cleaning, delivered by a team they trust.

Vision — To be the Twin Cities' most trusted name in cleaning — known equally for the quality of our work and the integrity of how we do it.
